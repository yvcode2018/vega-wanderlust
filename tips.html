Travel Tips
  • Research. Research. Research. Learn about the country, the culture, safe accommodations/points of interest. etc
  • Book flights 2-3 months in advance
  • Always get behind business travelers on security lines
  • Make a travel list
  • Pack extra outfit in carry-on
  • Make sure to take a jacket. Some nights can be chilly
  • Pack extra chargers
  • Flexibility is key
  • Make a copy of your passport
  • Take an extra bank card and credit card with you
  • Make sure to use no-fee bank/credit cards
  • Take about 200 of the local currency with you to your destination
  • Don't exchange all your money before you go (conversion fees are more expensive). When you get to your destination, look for the local bank and visit the ATM (some bank cards will have no fee or charge 3%)
  • Beware of your surroundings. Keep your belongings close to you and only take what is necessary
  • Learn basic phrases in the native language of your destination
  • Dine away from tourist attractions
  • Let your taste buds go crazy and try a dish that the country is known for
